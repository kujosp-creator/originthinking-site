The Origin Thinking™ System

Origin Thinking™ is one operating system, not a pile of programs. It is built to avoid Fake Lean: continuous improvement without real respect for or faith in people.

Start with the Book Browse OPS Tools™ See the Focus Deck

We put structure around problem solving and daily sustainment, and we put equal structure around leadership behavior. Until both change together, every Lean rollout is the same movie with a new title.

Four connected pieces

Each piece can stand alone, but they are built as one loop. Method and tools help you find the right answer, the leadership deck shapes how you lead through the change, and PROVE Loop™ plus the Tier 2 Help Chain keep improvements from sliding back into firefighting.

Most organizations treat problem solving, leadership development, and daily management as three separate efforts:

  • Problem solving lives in projects and events.
  • Leadership lives in training and slogans.
  • Daily management lives in huddles and dashboards.

The result is a broken loop. Solutions fade. Leaders revert. Huddles become status reports.

Origin Thinking™ is a single operating system that closes that loop. It connects:

  • Origin Problem Solving™ – how we define and fix problems.
  • OPS Tools™ – simple worksheets and visuals that support the method.
  • OPS Leadership Focus Deck™ – how leaders behave while we do the work.
  • PROVE Loop™ and Tier 2 Help Chain – how we check, every day, whether the new way of working is actually happening.

Layer 3 · Leadership

Origin Leadership Focus Deck™

The Origin Leadership Focus Deck™ (used inside the system as the OPS Leadership Focus Deck™) is an 18 card leadership tool for quarterly one to ones. Leaders sort the cards, choose one behavior to strengthen for the next 90 days, and write a short plan tied directly to real work. The focus lives in normal development notes and shows up in how the leader runs Go and See, huddles, and PROVE Loop™ checks, without turning the card itself into a public scorecard or performance rating.

Layer 4 · Sustain

PROVE Loop™

A short daily sustain routine at Tier 1, backed by a Tier 2 Help Chain. Under each metric, leaders run PROVE Loop™ in less than 5 minutes total: restate the gap, mark yesterday’s result, report what they saw at the work, set status Green, Yellow, or Red, and choose the next move or escalation. Red is a win because it catches drift early and keeps problems from sliding back into firefighting.

How to use the system

  1. Pick one real gap. Use Origin Problem Solving™ to define the target and actual, map the current condition, and design a better way of working with the people who do the work.
  2. Use the minimum OPS Tools™. Pull in only the tools that help you make the next decision, such as a Cause Map, Point of Cause Map, or A3 view. Do not fill out more fields than you need to see the problem clearly.
  3. Put the gap on the board. Add a PROVE Strip under the key metric on the Tier 1 board. Write the gap in the P box and set up a simple run chart in the R box so you can see the pattern every day.
  4. Run the daily PROVE Loop™. In the start of shift huddle, restate the gap, mark yesterday’s result, report what you saw at the work, set the status, and choose the next move. Escalate bigger obstacles to the Tier 2 Help Chain instead of letting them die in notebooks and email.
  5. Grow leaders on purpose. Once per quarter, use the Origin Leadership Focus Deck™ to choose one leadership behavior to practice. Capture that focus in the leader’s normal one to one or development notes and use it as a quiet lens during Go and See, daily work, and PROVE Loop™ checks.
  6. Make executive commitments explicit. Origin Thinking™ cannot be a side project. Executives agree to protect the work (huddles and Go and See time are not optional), protect people (Red shows system problems, not individual failure), and lead by example (they use the OPS Leadership Focus Deck™ themselves and carry a 90 day focus into their own one to ones and Go and See visits).
